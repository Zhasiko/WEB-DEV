Kaaba

Kaaba (Arabic لكعبة المرفة‎ - Al-Ka'batu-l-Musharrafa "Venerable Kaaba")
is a Muslim shrine in the form of a cubic building
in the courtyard of the al-Haram mosque (Reserved Mosque) in Mecca.

Mosque al-Haram

Al-Haram Mosque (Arabic: المسجد الحرام‎ - Al-Masjidu-l-Haram "Reserved or Forbidden Mosque") is the main and largest mosque in the world, in the inner courtyard of which is the main shrine of Islam - the Kaaba.
